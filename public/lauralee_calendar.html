Trips are occasionally cancelled due to forecast severe weather or rough seas. We try to give at least 12 hours notice for any cancellations. If the weather is questionable please call to confirm sailings.

Please arrive at least 15 minutes before sailing. Trips that are sold out may depart 10 minutes early.

You may cancel for a full refund with 24 hours notice, or a credit with 12 hours notice. We do not give refunds for no shows. If we cancel for any reason you will be put into our credit file until you let us know whether you prefer a refund or another date.

It is customary to tip the mates on a party boat. If you are satisfied with the service 20% of the paid fare is an appropriate tip. If you are a group of 6 or more we pay the mates tip (at no cost to you). If the service is not satisfactory please report it to the captain.
